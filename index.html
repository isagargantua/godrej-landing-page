About The Project

Only 151 exclusive residences across 2 landmark towers

Godrej Astra redefines ultra-luxury living in Gurgaon. Spanning 2.76 acres in Sector 54's most coveted corridor, the project introduces 151 meticulously planned residences with a single vision: low-density living with maximum privacy and scale.

Each home features a private lift lobby, expansive wraparound decks with skyline views, and access to a 40,000 sq ft clubhouse with infinity pool, wellness pavilion, and concierge services. Just 2–3 units per floor ensure an uncompromised address.
